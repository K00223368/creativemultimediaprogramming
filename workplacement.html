Creative Multimedia Programming

Work Placement

Work placement is carried out over the 3 months of the summer as part of the H.Dip Creative multimedia Programming. The work experience commences in June following the exams.

Most interenships are normally longer than 3 months. It varies from company to company. Some companies have internships anywhere from 9 to 12 months.

The objective of the student is to get work experience in order to be able to fulfill a job oportunity in the working environment.
